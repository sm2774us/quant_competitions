## 🗞️ Predicting Stock using News
Using natural language processing to extract market signals from news sentiment.

### Problem Description
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Quantifying the impact of news on stock prices.
- **NLP Pipelines**: Processing large volumes of news data in real-time.
- **Multi-Modal Data**: Combining news sentiment with historical price data.
